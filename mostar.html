Exploring Mostar!

A day trip into the bustling town of Mostar in Bosnia & Herzegovina

June 6, 2025 Rory MacGinty

Crossing the border of Croatia into Bosnia & Herzegovina to visit the beautiful town of Mostar. The picteresque, quaint old town of Mostar was busy with tourists and locals mixing together over food and drink. The rich history of the town is a lot more recent than you would think with evidence of the destruction that took place there not too long ago just a short walk away from the main tourist area.
